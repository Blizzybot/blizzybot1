GI Joe

G.I. Joe is an elite international military task force made up of the best soldiers, pilots, spies, medics, and tech experts in the world.

Qualities

  1. Bravery
  2. Teamwork
  3. Discipline
  4. Honor
  5. Loyalty
  6. Freedom
  7. Justice
  8. Protecting the world from evil

Skillset

  1. Advanced weapons & vehicles
  2. Teamwork and strategy
  3. Specific specialty (snipers, ninjas, medics, engineers, etc.)
  4. Technical Proficiency
  5. Leadership Skills

Catchphrase

"Yo Joe!"
